Toxic Comment Classification Challenge

Description

For this project, I was responsible for preprocessing the raw comments from Wikipedia and designing the models. To be trainable, the raw comments were processed to remove duplicating words, punctuations, numbers, ..., and were turned into sequences of integers. Then, we focused to analyze the application of the article Retrofitting Word Vectors to Semantic Lexicons to this task as comments can be implicitly toxic. Our best score for this challege is 0.97621 with details as in the presentation below.
