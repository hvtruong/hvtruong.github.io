Safe Restaurants

Description

Safe Restaurants was designed following the singleton pattern. The application uses and updates with latest inspections from Surrey's Open data. The green, yellow, red markers are corresponding to low, moderate, high hazard levels where users can click to observe detailed violations. With this application, users can find safe restaurants nearby with ease or search for their favourite restaurants. Moreover, we also allow users to mark their favourite restaurants and apply this feature while searching.
For this project, my main works were to display the restaurants as markers on Google Maps and cluster them. I also developed the search engine to filter out restaurants that do not match user's needs.
